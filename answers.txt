collaborate effectively with team members, and maintain a complete history of changes.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merge creates a new "merge commit" to combine divergent branch histories. Rebase, on the other hand, moves a branch's commits to the top of another branch.
